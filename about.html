About Retail Nexus

Our Project

Retail Nexus is an AI-powered Retail Management System designed to simplify retail operations. It helps businesses manage products, inventory, customers, and sales through a single platform. The system provides real-time insights, improves decision-making, and enhances customer satisfaction using AI-based recommendations and analytics.

Key Features

📦 Product Management

Add, update, and manage products easily.

📊 Sales Analytics

Track sales performance with reports and dashboards.

🤖 AI Assistance

Get smart recommendations and retail insights.

📈 Inventory Tracking

Monitor stock levels and receive low-stock alerts.
